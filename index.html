Everybody quotes John 3:16. But ask a hundred Christians what eternal life means and you'll hear the same answer: a destination. A later thing. A forever-after thing.
JOHN 3:16
The invitation. God so loved the world.
JOHN 17:3
The definition. "This IS eternal life — that they KNOW You." Not later. Now.
MATTHEW 7:23
The warning. "Away from me — I never knew you."
Jesus defined it Himself. The night before the cross, praying to His Father, He said eternal life is knowing God. It's not a someday address. It's a relationship that starts breathing right now.
And Matthew 7:23 is the warning label. People who taught, served, sat in church thirty years, even got baptized — and heard "I never knew you." They performed for Him. They never knew Him. Performance was never the point.
That's why Know God Better exists. Your sin is not your identity. Your past doesn't define you. We solve one problem — hopelessness — with one solution: knowing Jesus, through daily engagement with the Holy Spirit. From hopeless to hope-filled.

How this started

An old hippie and a $500-a-day habit.

I was 19 and out of options. A $500-a-day habit I couldn't stop on my own. I didn't want to keep living the way I was living. Truth is, I didn't want to live at all.
An old hippie in AA asked me one question. "You got a problem with God?" I said no. He said, "Great. Get on your knees every morning and ask God to keep you sober. At night, tell Him thank you." No church background. No religion. I was just desperate enough to follow instructions. That was 1991. I've been sober ever since.
God wasn't done. The Holy Spirit spent the next nine years drawing me toward Jesus. In 2000, I gave my life to Christ — a heart choice, not a church habit. I didn't get to know God through religion. I got to know Him one honest prayer at a time.
That's exactly how this app works. One honest prayer at a time. If you're where I was — broken, out of options, no church language — you're the reason this exists. The door is open. Come on in. You're welcome anytime.
